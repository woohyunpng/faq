  • GO Info

  • How do GOs work?

    I ask the seller or fansite if they are open to overseas orders and if there are any other GOs for the US. If there is a GO already I will not open one. Please don't request (if requests are open) if something already has a US GOM!

    Once I get the okay, I make a tweet and once the details are finalized (i.e. how much the goods will cost, etc.) then I open a form.

    The form will usually close 1 or 2 days before the deadline just in case anything goes wrong with my deposit. If there is a minimum and I don’t hit it, then I refund everyone. Once the deadline arrives, I close the form and send the 1st payment.

    The seller or fansite will then have the goods in production and when they’re done they will ship to my Korean address. Once at my Korean address, I’ll get an EMS estimate which I will split among the buyers.

    As soon as every buyer pays their share, I will send the payment and the goods will be shipped to my house in California. From there, I will see how much domestic or international shipping is and send out emails about the fees to each buyer.

    Once domestic shipping is paid, I’ll finally ship the goods to each buyer~

    Sometimes if things have been delayed already, I'll pay for EMS/DHL out of pocket and then send out fee emails for that.

  • Do you have a Korean address? How do Korean addresses work

    I use Korgou as my Korean address. It is a warehouse where goods arrive and are placed in my inventory where they wait until I am ready to combine orders for a shipment to my house.

    Korgou provides me with an address that I use to fill out forms. Sometimes I will also give that address out (such as in the case of fanclub membership GOs) so that others can also benefit from combining shipments to reduce EMS.

  • Do you have a Korean bank account? Is it really your own?

    Yes, I have a "Korean bank account" but it does not mean it's my own account. I use Korgou and SendHow to send payments via bank transfer to sellers and fansites.

  • What does GO/GOM/IO/EMS/DHL mean?

    GO stands for group order.
    GOMs are group order managers
    IO stands for individual order
    EMS is Express Mail Service and it is a shipping service
    DHL is also a shipping service that is very similar to EMS. Both EMS and DHL are fast (2-3 business days) but can get pricey.

  • Individual Orders

  • What are individual orders/IOs?

    Individual orders (I've also heard them called one-offs and personal orders) are orders where instead of multiple people involved there is only 1 (or 2 sometimes).

  • How are IOs different from GOs?

    IOs are less formal than GOs. There is no form for each item, rather you use the general individual order form to place an order and the payment info will be sent in the confirmation email.

    Unlike GOs, the price for an item in an IO will include the domestic shipping fee within Korea (i.e. the fee for the item to be delivered to my warehouse) and the bank transfer fee.

    There is no minimum for joining an IO but there is a maximum which will be on my spreadsheet

  • What's the IO process? How do they work?

    The process goes like this

    1) An individual order is placed - Please make sure it is well before the deadline and if the deadline is on the weekend in Korea, the latest you can submit an order for it is Thursday (in California time) of that week. Please keep timezones in mind!!

    2) I go over your order and determine if it's possible (i.e. is there stock, is the deadline reasonable). If it is not, I'll send you a DM about it. If it is possible you will get a confirmation email with the price of the goods in USD and the domestic shipping in Korea (which is usually around $3)

    3) Once I receive the money, I deposit and place the order.

    4) Once goods arrive at my Korean address, I wait until there are enough other goods so that EMS is cheap for you. Previous individual orders I've done have had EMS around $2 - $3. This means your order might take longer than normal (especially if I have to wait for more orders to reach my K-address). Please keep this in mind when you order. Please do not ask me for a time estimate because I can't give you one.

    5) Once I know EMS, I send you an email. After it's paid, I have the items shipped to me

    6) When items are at my US house I send another email with a link to the domestic shipping form. After you fill it out I'll send you an email with the different rates. Once you pay your desired rate, I package and ship your item!

  • How long do IOs take?

    This will depend on the goods ordered. If you ordered a doll, for example, it takes between 4 to 6 months. If you ordered a slogan, it can take between 1 to 2 months. Stickers and other small items can take up to a month.

    In addition to this, it can take up to a week or two after the IO has arrived at my Korean warehouse for it to ship to my house because I have to wait until there are enough other goods so EMS/DHL is cheap for you.

  • I'm worried about EMS fees- are they high for IOs?

  • Payments

  • What are my payment options?

    I accept PayPal and Venmo only. I only accept Friends and Family on PayPal unless your country does not allow that option- in which case please let me know BEFORE you pay or I will refund your payment.

  • Do I have to pay right away when submitting a form?

    No, you have until the deadline to pay. If you do not pay before the deadline your order will be dropped. Do not ask to make an exception.

    However, if you talk to me BEFORE the deadline and let me know you'll need more time to pay, I will give you an extension that works with your circumstances, but you MUST pay before that extension or else I will drop you from the order and that will count as a strike against you.

  • Why do I have to use Friends and Family?

    The IRS has been known to target accounts that receive a lot of Goods and Services payments and sometimes PayPal locks accounts which means the money there cannot be used. This is why I insist on Friends and Family for buyers from the US (and other countries that support it).

  • What happens if I miss a payment?

    If you miss the 1st payment without letting me know your circumstances, then you are dropped from the order. If you missed the 2nd payment without letting me know your circumstances, I will withhold your goods and you have to wait until I sell them in order to get a refund. This will also count as a strike against you.

    If you asked for an extention before the deadline and then you also missed that 2nd deadline (after I have paid for your order) then I will put your goods up for reclaim and you will be placed on a banned list.

    Do not take advantage of my kindness.

  • I don't want the item I ordered anymore, can I get a refund?

    If you ask for a refund BEFORE the deadline, yes. If it is AFTER the deadline, then you have to wait until someone else buys your order.

  • I sent the wrong payment/double payment- can I get a refund for the excess?

    Yes! Please reply to your confirmation email with proof of the incorrect payment. Do note that refunds are slow because I have a lot of things to take care of.

  • Can I pay in installments?

    I will allow installments on a case to case basis and you must have ordered with me at least twice and have good standing (no strikes).

  • Shipping

  • How much will the EMS/DHL fee be?

    The exact number will depend on the weight of the goods and the number of people in the combined orders I am shipping. It will always be under $5.

  • How much is domestic (within the US) shipping?

    Medial mail for albums and photobooks starts around $2.63. First class shipping (slogans, other lightweight items) starts at $4. Priority shipping (heavier items) starts at $6. All rates go up with weight and with distance from Los Angeles (where I ship from).

  • How much is international shipping?

    Rates start between $15 (for lightweight objects) and $20 and go up with weight and country being shipped to. I cannot give you a more accurate estimate :(

  • How do you ship goods? What service do you use?

    I use USPS by default for both domestic and international shipping- I can also check rates for other services (FedEx, UPS, DHL) but rates for those will take longer.

  • My items have arrived at your house but I haven't received the domestic shipping form yet?

    Please check that I have sent the form by checking my email tag. If emails have been sent then please check your spam. Lastly make sure you paid for EMS/DHL before the deadline- if you did not then your order has been forfeited and you need to wait until I sell it in order to get your refund.

  • I have not gotten a domestic rate email?
